## üé® PowerPoint Design Tips

**Color Scheme:**
- Primary: Blue (#3b82f6) - matches your UI
- Secondary: Dark gray (#1f2937) for text
- Accent: Green (#10b981) for highlights
- Background: White or light gray

**Fonts:**
- Headings: Bold, 32-44pt
- Body: Regular, 18-24pt
- Code/Technical: Monospace, 16-20pt

**Layout:**
- Keep 40% white space
- Maximum 5 bullets per slide
- Use icons for visual interest
- One main idea per slide
